## 2. Core Services & Packages

### A. Corporate Services (Firmenbetreuung)
- **Target:** Entrepreneurs wanting to incorporate in Dubai or Spain.
- **Dubai Benefits:** 
  - 0% Corporate Tax for Qualifying Income up to AED 375,000 (~€95k).
  - 9% Corporate Tax above that threshold (still very low compared to DE ~45%).
  - 100% Foreign Ownership allowed in Freezones.
  - No personal income tax in UAE.
- **Spain Benefits:**
  - Access to EU market.
  - Holding structures (ETVE) for international tax planning.
- **Pricing:** Packages start from €999 (placeholder).

### B. Dubai & Golden Visa
- **Dubai Residency:**
  - Obtained via company formation or property investment.
  - Valid for 2-10 years (renewable).
  - Required for tax residency status.
- **Spanish Golden Visa:**
  - Residency by investment (e.g., €500k in real estate).
  - Allows travel within Schengen Zone.
  - Path to citizenship after 10 years (2 years for Ibero-American citizens).

### C. Insolvency Services (Insolvenz & Schuldenbereinigung)
- **Focus:** EU Insolvency Law / Spanish "Ley de Segunda Oportunidad".
- **Comparison (DE vs. ES):**
  - **Germany:** Strict procedures, long "Wohlverhaltensphase" (good conduct period) of 3 years.
  - **Spain:** Often faster discharge of debt (Restschuldbefreiung) for individuals.
  - **Strategy:** Moving COMI (Center of Main Interests) to Spain to file for insolvency there under EU regulations.
- **Target:** Entrepreneurs with personal liability debts seeking a fresh start.

## 3. Tax Optimization Details (The "Numbers")

### "Beckham Law" (Spain)
- **What is it?** Special expat tax regime (Régimen de Impatriados).
- **Rate:** Flat 24% on Spanish-sourced income up to €600,000.
- **Foreign Income:** Generally NOT taxed in Spain (unlike standard tax residents who pay on worldwide income).
- **Duration:** Available for the year of arrival + 5 subsequent years (Total 6 years).
- **Who qualifies?** Must not have been tax resident in Spain for the last 5 years. Moving for a job or as a director of a company (with <25% ownership if it's an asset-holding entity, but rules are flexible for active companies).

### Dubai Freezone Tax
- **0% Rate:** Applies to "Qualifying Income" (e.g., transactions with other Freezone persons, or passive income).
- **9% Rate:** Applies to "Non-Qualifying Income" (e.g., trading with mainland UAE without a mainland branch).
- **Small Business Relief:** 0% tax if revenue is below AED 3M (~€750k) for tax periods ending before Dec 31, 2026.
