  **Design Movement**: "Titanium Glass Minimalism"
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Materiality**: The interface should feel like it's made of physical, high-end materials: frosted glass, brushed titanium, and deep obsidian.
  2.  **Depth**: Extreme use of depth (z-index), blurs, and shadows to create a sense of space and exclusivity.
  3.  **Restraint**: Less is more. Only the most essential information is shown initially; details reveal themselves on interaction.
  4.  **Precision**: Every pixel, every border, every animation must be mathematically perfect, like a Swiss watch.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Obsidian Black (#050505)**: The base. Deep, infinite, absorbing light.
  -   **Titanium Grey (#2A2A2A)**: For structural elements. Cool, strong, industrial but refined.
  -   **Platinum White (#E5E5E5)**: For text. Not harsh pure white, but a soft, glowing platinum.
  -   **Accent**: No "colors". The accent is light itself (white glow, reflections).

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Bento Grids**: Content organized in highly structured, glass-like containers (like Apple's control center but darker and more premium).
  -   **Center-Stage**: The main value proposition is always center stage, with everything else fading into the background.
  -   **Fluid Typography**: Text size scales perfectly with the viewport, feeling "printed" on the screen.

  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Frosted Glass Cards**: High backdrop-blur, thin white borders (1px), subtle noise texture.
  -   **Titanium Borders**: Gradients on borders that simulate metallic reflection.
  -   **Spotlight Effects**: Mouse movement reveals content through a "flashlight" effect on the glass.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Weighty Animations**: Elements don't just "appear"; they slide in with mass and momentum.
  -   **Magnetic Buttons**: Buttons that pull the cursor slightly, feeling tactile.

  **Animation**:
  -   **Slow Parallax**: Background elements move very slowly, creating a subconscious sense of depth.
  -   **Blur-In**: Content blurs into focus rather than fading in.

  **Typography System**:
  -   **Headlines**: "SF Pro Display" or "Inter" (tight tracking, heavy weight) - The Apple standard.
  -   **Body**: "SF Pro Text" or "Inter" (normal tracking, light weight).
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: "Invisible Tech"

  -   **Silver (#C0C0C0)**: For secondary elements.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Free Flow**: Elements are placed organically, not in a rigid grid.
  -   **Floating UI**: Navigation and tools float above the content.

  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Glowing Orbs**: Abstract, blurred orbs of light in the background.
  -   **Glass Morphism**: Heavy use of glass for all UI elements.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Liquid Motion**: Elements morph into each other.
  -   **Gaze Tracking**: (Simulated) elements react to where the user is likely looking.

  **Animation**:
  -   **Morphing**: Shapes change form fluidly.
  -   **Ripple Effects**: Clicks create ripples.

  -   **Headlines**: "Uncut Sans" or "Satoshi".
